AI-Automated LinkedIn Voice Messages

Outbound Hack by Aura

LinkedIn voice notes have 3x higher response rates than text. AI lets you send them at scale.

Why Voice Notes Work

  • Stand out in crowded inboxes
  • Feel personal and human
  • Build rapport faster
  • Hard to ignore

AI Voice Cloning Process

  1. Record samples: 3-5 minutes of natural speech
  2. Train model: Use ElevenLabs or Play.ht
  3. Create templates: Scripts with personalization variables
  4. Generate audio: AI creates custom messages
  5. Send via automation: Integrate with LinkedIn tools

Best Practices

  • Keep messages under 60 seconds
  • Sound natural, not scripted
  • Use their name early
  • Reference something specific
  • Clear call-to-action

Example Script

"Hey {{first_name}}, quick voice note for you. I noticed {{company}} is growing the sales team, and I thought you might be interested in how we helped {{similar_company}} book 40% more meetings. No pressure, but if you're curious, happy to share what worked for them. Let me know!"
